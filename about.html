Where It All Began

I’m a BCA student at Amity University, Noida, and I’m passionate about creating things that look good and work even better. As a Frontend Developer and UI/UX Designer, I love turning ideas into reality — from clean, minimal designs to interactive user experiences that feel just right.

My journey into tech started with pure curiosity. I’d see websites and wonder, “How does this even work?” That curiosity slowly turned into passion, and today, I’m here, building websites that aren’t just visually appealing but also smooth and functional.

I believe that design isn’t just about looks — it’s about how something feels. That’s why I focus on both logic and creativity. Whether it's creating pixel-perfect designs or ensuring every button click feels satisfying, I’m always pushing myself to do better.
